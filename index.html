Experience world-class diving in Anilao, hike scenic mountain trails, explore pristine beaches, and immerse yourself in the rich culture of Batangas.

Plan Your Visit

Explore Mabini's Natural Wonders

Anilao Diving Spots

Explore Anilao’s reefs, macro critters, and clear waters—long hailed as the Philippines’ dive capital.

Mt. Gulugod Baboy

Day-hike its rolling ridgeline for sweeping views of Batangas Bay, Maricaban, and Verde Island Passage.

Sea Spring Resort

Unwind in saltwater pools, hot spring baths, and calm coves framed by Sombrero and Maricaban island views.

Local Delicacies

Slurp a hearty bowl of Batangas lomi—rich noodles with chicharon—and other hometown staples loved by locals.

Culture & Local Life

Marine Conservation

Local communities work together to preserve the rich marine biodiversity that makes Anilao famous worldwide.

Culinary Heritage

Traditional cooking methods and family recipes passed down through generations create unforgettable flavors.

Island Traditions

Experience the warm hospitality and vibrant festivals that celebrate Mabini's coastal heritage.
